material-checkbox

Usage

    <material-checkbox label="Subscribe"></material-checkbox>

Attributes

  • label: String, label on the right side of the checkbox
  • checked: empty, setting this attribute will check the checkbox

Styling

  • color of radiobutton when not checked: --unchecked-color
  • color of radiobutton when checked: --checked-color
  • color of label: --label-color
